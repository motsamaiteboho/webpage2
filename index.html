Historic places

Anglo Boer War Museum

This museum is dedicated to the historical war between British and the Boers. The museum tells the tragic tale of the war, its effect on the women, children and society in general.

Naval Hill

To have a panoramic view of the city of roses, visit Naval hill which gets its name because of the two British naval guns standing on the hill since early 20th century.

Hertzog Square

Dedicated to the South African general, Politician, Prime minister and national figure James Barry Munnik Hertzog, the Hertzog square is one of the prime hangouts in Bloemfontein.
